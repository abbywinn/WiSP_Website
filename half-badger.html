HALF BADGER

The Half Badger is the Wisconsin Space Program's first venture into liquid rocketry and is scheduled to launch at the 2026 FAR-OUT competition. This project sets the technical foundation for our next objective: developing a liquid rocket capable of reaching the Kármán line.
