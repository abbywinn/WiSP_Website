HALF BADGER

The Half Badger is the Wisconsin Space Program's first venture into liquid rocketry, and is set to fly at the 2026 FAR-OUT competition in Mojave, California. This project sets the technical foundation for our next objective: creating a liquid rocket capable of reaching the Káráman line at 100 kilometers above sea level.
