Wisconsin Space
Program

The Wisconsin Space Program aims to be the first student team to fly a liquid rocket to the edge of space.

Engineers at WiSP design, analyze, manufacture, test, and launch high-powered rocketry systems. We compete in large intercollegiate rocketry competitions, as well as do long-term development on propulsion systems.
